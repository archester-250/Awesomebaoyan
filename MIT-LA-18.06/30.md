# SVD

![image-20230423152212380](images/image-20230423152212380.png)
$$
AV=U\Sigma
$$

$$
A=U\Sigma V^{-1}=U\Sigma V^T
$$

$U$和$V$都是正交矩阵

![image-20230423152819502](images/image-20230423152819502.png)

$V$是$A^TA$的特征向量，$U$是$AA^T$的特征向量
